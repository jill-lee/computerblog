If there were two words to desrcibe me it would be creativity and perfection. Perfection in a way, I wanted everything in my life to be molded into a certain way I liked. Growing up in the early they say I was already two steps ahead of everyone else, shaped to be multitalented and academically succesful kid, my life revolved around being a high achiever. People would ask me if I get tired from all the studying and extra curriculars. I shake my head and say no. And so the only coping mechanism I had with the burden to do my best was art. Art was a release, I remembered how as a kid I would always draw anywhere with anything, it didn't matter what medium it was, as long as I was using my creative side and expressing it through shapes and colors. So despite the hardships and burnouts I went through for being pushed to be the greatest I can be, I was happy.
